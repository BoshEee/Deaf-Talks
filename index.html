Deafs Talks

Welcome to deafs talk! With this app you can talk safly with your deaf partner while you are driving!

How It Works?

While you are driving you can't use sign language to talk with your partner, or look at your partner while they using sign language and loose your focus on the road.

Deafs Talks helps you to speak to them by clicking a button and recording what you want to say and display it on the screen so your partner can read it. Then, your partner can type what they want to say and click the Read button and you will hear what they typed without loosing your focus.
